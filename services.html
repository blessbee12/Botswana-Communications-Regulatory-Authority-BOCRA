What we offer

Comprehensive regulatory services for Botswana's communications sector

BOCRA provides licensing, spectrum management, consumer protection, type approval, postal regulation, and universal access services to ensure fair competition, quality, and inclusion across Botswana.

Core Services

Our Regulatory Mandate in Action

Telecommunications Licensing

Licensing and regulation of mobile, fixed-line, and data service providers. Setting interconnection rates and quality of service standards.

Broadcasting Services

Oversight of radio and television broadcasters. Content standards, licensing, and ensuring diverse Botswana-relevant programming.

Spectrum Management

Management of Botswana's radio frequency spectrum. Licensing, monitoring, and interference resolution.

Consumer Protection

Handling complaints, publishing quality benchmarks, and ensuring operators meet obligations to Batswana consumers.

Type Approval

Certification of communications equipment for use in Botswana. Mandatory for all imported and sold devices.

Postal Services

Regulation of Botswana Post and private courier operators. Universal postal service obligations and quality standards.

How to apply

Application Process

01

Submit Application

Complete the required form with supporting documents and pay the initial fee.

02

Assessment

BOCRA reviews completeness, compliance, and conducts background checks.

03

Inspection

Technical inspection and verification of facilities and equipment.

04

Issuance

Final decision, license issuance, and entry into BOCRA registry.

Licensing Fees

Fee Structure 2025/2026

Transparent fee structure for all licensing categories. All fees are payable in Botswana Pula (BWP).

P5,000 / year
Individual License

For individual practitioners and small operators

  • Application fee: P500
  • Processing: 30 days
  • Annual renewal required
  • Basic compliance reporting
P25,000 / year
Class License

For established telecommunications operators

  • Application fee: P2,500
  • Processing: 60-90 days
  • Full spectrum access
  • Priority support
P50,000 / year
Broadcast License

For radio and television broadcasters

  • Application fee: P5,000
  • Processing: 90 days
  • Content compliance review
  • Quarterly reporting
